How to Install

1

Download the APK

Tap the Download APK button above. The file ScanSplit.apk will be saved to your Downloads folder.

2

Allow Unknown Sources

When prompted, go to Settings → Install Unknown Apps and enable it for your browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.).

3

Install the App

Open the downloaded ScanSplit.apk file and tap Install. Android may show a Play Protect warning — tap "Install Anyway".

4

Sign In & Start Scanning

Open ScanSplit, sign in with your Google account, and start scanning your first UPI screenshot!
